Far Horizon Holdings

Building thriving futures across seven generations through purpose-driven online businesses that strengthen families and communities.

Our Mission

At Far Horizon Holdings, we believe that the decisions we make today shape the world our descendants will inherit seven generations from now. This long-term perspective—spanning approximately 140 years—guides every investment, every business venture, and every strategic decision we make.

We are a Wyoming-based holding company that creates and acquires online businesses with a shared purpose: helping families thrive across generations. Our portfolio companies address the fundamental needs that ensure not just survival, but flourishing—from financial resilience and educational empowerment to community building and sustainable living.

Seven-Generation Thinking

Every decision is evaluated through the lens of its impact on future generations, ensuring sustainable and meaningful growth.

Family-Centered Impact

We focus on strengthening the bonds, resources, and capabilities that help families thrive together across time.

Digital Innovation

Leveraging online platforms and digital tools to create scalable solutions that can reach and serve families worldwide.

Our Portfolio

Our growing portfolio of businesses reflects our commitment to creating comprehensive support systems for multigenerational family success. Each venture is carefully selected or developed to address critical areas where families need reliable, accessible resources.

Financial Empowerment

Businesses focused on helping families build generational wealth, understand money management, and create financial stability that spans decades.

Educational Resources

Platforms that support lifelong learning, skills development, and knowledge transfer between generations within families.

Health & Wellness

Solutions that promote physical, mental, and emotional wellbeing for all family members across different life stages.

Community Building

Tools and platforms that strengthen connections within families and help them build supportive community networks.

Legacy Preservation

Sustainable Living

Resources that help families make environmentally conscious choices and prepare for long-term sustainability.
